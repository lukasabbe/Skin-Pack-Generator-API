Create Custom Minecraft Statues from Player Skins

Turn carved pumpkins into personalized player statues with just a few clicks!
This resource-pack generator lets you input Minecraft usernames, then easily download a custom resource pack. Rename a carved pumpkin in-game in an anvil, and the pumkin will become a statue of that skin

How to use

1. Input the Minecraft usernames you want to create statues for, separated by spaces.
2. Click the "Generate Skin Pack" button.
3. Wait for the pack to be generated.
4. Place it in your Minecraft resource packs folder.
5. Open Minecraft and select the pack in the resource packs menu.
6. Rename a carved pumpkin in an anvil to the name of the skin you want to use.
7. Enjoy your custom player statues in-game!

Note: The pack will be generated in a queue, so please be patient.
